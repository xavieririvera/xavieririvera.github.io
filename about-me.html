About Me
Strategic, impactful, human-centered designer
My love for design started with my dad's old camera. As a kid I loved to compare my perspective to that which I created and manipulated with the camera's manual controls. I took this natural inclination to create and applied it to computer-aided design, marketing, and eventually in the business world.

Now, my passion is building solutions to challenging problems at the intersection of data and design. I apply design thinking, technical know-how, and intuition to create new approaches to transform organizations, brands, and campaigns.

At Georgetown, I'm finishing up a Political Economy major, with a Business Administration Minor and Entrepreneurship Fellowship. I'll be graduating in May 2019 and am looking for part-time and summer opportunities.
