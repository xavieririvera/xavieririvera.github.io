Local Highlights

"Growing up in Minnesota, I always knew that I wanted to be in a city as dynamic and international as D.C. There is so much to offer and I'm still discovering new parts of the city. If you're only here for a few days, here are some places you have to hit:

Adams Morgan

A vibrant college go-to for fun at night, this is a staple of the DC College life. Bars, clubs, the indie bands playing on every corner, and amazing food, AdMo is a local favorite.

The Tidal Basin

While a trip to D.C. isn't complete with a photo in front of the Lincoln Memorial, make sure not to skip out on the Tidal Basin. While fall isn't the season for its classic cherry blossoms, the Tidal Basin is a must-see all year round.

Echostage

A part of town that every college student in the Capitol knows well, make sure to check out the music scene here. Any given weekend, there's bound to be a concert you won't want to miss!"
