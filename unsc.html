Meeting of the United Nations Security Council , 1991

The world is at a turning point. With fires constantly cropping up around the world, the world's most powerful nations are tasked with putting them out, before a new one sparks. Danger could come from all corners of the world, in all shapes and sizes. The moves made in this committee room will be amplified through the years. Be smart. Be careful.
