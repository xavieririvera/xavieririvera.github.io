Outlistened, Fall 2018
Designing a feedforward digital strategy to REACH your customerbase online
Bringing digital marketing success stories to entrepreneurs to supercharge their online strategy through quick wins
The first thing I ever marketed was a neighborhood translation service. Designing print and web advertisements, I went through the painstaking process of developing a brand identity and online strategy retroactively. Since then, I've worked with marketing teams for some of the world's biggest brands and fastest growing companies.

Now, I want to take what I've learned and create an actionable book for small business owners and non-profit organizations to supercharge their digital marketing. In Fall 2018, I will publish Outlistened with New Degree Press. This book is written for small businesses and NGOs to leverage industry-leading digital strategy techniques used by the pros. I break down how to build an online presence through feedforward digital strategy frameworks to reach your customer-base.  
Interview Process
Currently, I am interviewing entrepreneurs, consultants, and marketing professionals to collect stories from their careers and learn about their most difficult challenges in reaching customer-bases online.

I intend the interviews to be a win-win process for me as a writer and for the interviewee to use this book as a platform to share their stories and ideas, prominently featuring your name in the book as quick case studies and stories.
