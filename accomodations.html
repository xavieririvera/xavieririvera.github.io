Accomodations

NCSC XLV will be held at the newly-renovated Hyatt Regency in Bethesda, Maryland, one of DC’s most vibrant suburbs. Mere feet away from the Metro, this prime location allows for easy access to our nation’s capital--known for its rich history and vibrant nightlife.

We have secured a special rate for conference attendees of $189/night for double-double rooms.

If your delegation has any questions regarding hotel reservation, please contact our Director of Delegate Affairs, Avery League, at ncscregistration@modelun.org.

Explore the renovations and more at https://bethesda.regency.hyatt.com/en/hotel/home.html.
