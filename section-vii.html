Section VII, 1945

Britain has fallen! At Dunkirk, the Wehrmacht deployed a frightening new array of tactics and weapons to successfully annihilate the remnants of the French Army and the British Expeditionary Force. With the British Home Fleet fatally wounded in the battle, Germany invaded the defenseless isle. Six months later, the Royal Family has fled to Canada, the Wehrmacht has occupied the isle up to Scottish highlands, and the government has taken up refuge in the remote town of Fort Williams. In response, the government did the unthinkable--activate Section VII. Now it falls to the Auxiliary Units and scattered resistance forces to continue the fight all while the government contends with managing its Empire and its surviving forces in Africa and Asia. The war has come to Britain, and she will not rest until the Germans are beaten down and the royal standard again flies over Buckingham Palace!
