Years of Lead, 1969

Benvenuto, delegates, to the Years of Lead – the Anni di plombo in Italy marked a period of intense societal conflict, with extremist Marxist, fascist, right-wing terrorism, Cold War ideological tensions, changing labor and social relations, and whispers of international conspiracies and coups. As the ministers of Italy’s cabinet during this decade of turmoil, you will face terrorist attacks, conspiracies, and rising political cleavages – and it won’t help that because of Italy’s parliamentary system, you will have to maintain political coalitions with your fellow delegates to keep yourselves in power. The complex coalition politics of the period caused much of the social tension as the neo-fascist MSI party and the reactionary Communist Party attempted to gain governing roles. Your internal politics will be the subject of international scrutiny, and it will be your job to prevent outside support to terrorist groups--or to aid and abet them, if you so choose.
