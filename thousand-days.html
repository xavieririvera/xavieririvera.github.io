The Thousand Days War JCC, 1899

Conservatives v. Liberals

As the 20th century dawns, the Republic of Columbia finds itself yet again embroiled in civil conflict. For decades, liberals and conservatives have battled for control of the state, and in 1899 the tension finally escalated to a civil armed conflict that would be known as the 1000 Days' War. Years of battle, coup d'Ã©tats, economic uncertainty, and rebellion, both within present day Columbia and the Department of Panama, would ensue. In this dynamic committee, generals, advisors, and political leaders on both the liberal and conservative sides of the conflict will fight to shape the uncertain future of Columbia, all while navigating the different crises caused by this complex war. Who will triumph in this struggle for power and unity?
